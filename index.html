About me

I am currently a graduate student in university of washington studying chemical engineering, and my research topic is the application of data science in the organic photovoltaic development.

I gained my bachelor degree on petroleum engineering in Colorado School of Mine. The reason I switched major was because I am more interested in the development and deployment of renewable energy. I have done two projects during the DIRECT (data intensive training program) offered by CEI (Clean Energy Institute) in UW. My first project was about quantitative relationship between molecular structure and properties, and the second capstone project was estimation of possible total solar potential in the area of city of Anchorage, Alaska

In order to gain deeper understanding in computer science and a broader scope, I come to this coding bootcamp and wish to learn.
